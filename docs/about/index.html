About Us

Welcome to Ootru Bikes!

At Ootru Bikes, we are passionate about motorbikes and committed to providing you with the most comprehensive and up-to-date information on all motorbikes available in the Indian market. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a newcomer, our platform is designed to help you find the perfect bike that suits your needs and preferences.

What We Offer

Our website allows you to explore a wide range of motorbikes, with detailed specifications and features. You can easily sort and view bikes based on various criteria such as:

  • Price: Find bikes that fit your budget.
  • Engine Capacity (CC): Choose from a variety of engine sizes.
  • Power: Compare bikes based on their horsepower.
  • Gear: Select bikes with the gear system you prefer.
  • Speed: Discover the top speeds of different models.
  • Tank Capacity: Check out bikes with the fuel capacity that meets your requirements.

Our Technology

Our platform is built using Angular, ensuring a fast, responsive, and user-friendly experience. We leverage the latest web technologies to provide you with a seamless browsing experience, whether you're on a desktop, tablet, or mobile device.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower you with the information you need to make informed decisions about your next motorbike purchase. We strive to be your go-to resource for all things related to motorbikes in India.

Thank you for visiting Ootru Bikes. We hope you find our website helpful and informative. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Happy riding!
